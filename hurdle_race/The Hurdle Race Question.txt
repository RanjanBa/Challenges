Dan is playing a video game in which his character competes in a hurdle
race by jumping over "n" hurdles with heights h0, h1,h2, h3, .... , h(n-1) .
He can initially jump a maximum height of k units, but he has an unlimited
supply of magic beverages that help him jump higher!Each time Dan drinks a magic
beverage, the maximum height he can jump during the race increases by unit.

Given n, k, and the heights of all the hurdles, find and print the minimum number of magic
beverages Dan must drink to complete the race.
